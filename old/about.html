Being a photographer is more than a hobby for me. When I have that camera up to my eye, the rest of the world fades away and it's all about the subject in front of me. I live for the rush that happens when I get that perfect shot, when I know I've captured something special and meaningful. Photographs speak volumes and I want the ones I take for you to bring back your favorite memories as though they happened yesterday.

I'm available to shoot engagements, portraits, headshots, family photos, pet portraits and more! My home base is Berkeley, CA but if you're willing to pay for gas, I'm willing to travel to you!
